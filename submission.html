Paper Submission

ICCT-SD-26 invites researchers, practitioners, and students worldwide to submit previously unpublished original papers for consideration for presentation at the conference in English.

Submission Guidelines

1

Double-blind peer review will be applied to every submission.

2

Each paper is limited to 8-10 pages. All the submitted papers will undergo plagiarism check by Turnitin/iThenticate before undergoing review process. Only papers that are both accepted and presented will be published in the conference proceedings.

3

Plagiarism should be less than 15%. Otherwise the paper will not be considered for review and rejected directly.

4

Authors must prepare papers using the prescribed Springer conference template listed below, follow all formatting and writing guidelines, and ensure the manuscript is grammatically correct and language-checked before submission, as non-compliance may lead to rejection by Springer.

5

Each paper must include an abstract of 200-250 words and 4-5 relevant keywords.

6

Figures and tables must be original and should not be copied from any previously published work or online source; copied content will result in rejection.

7

Every table and figure should be properly numbered and cited within the text. All figures must be of high resolution.

8

All reference numbers must be accurately cited in the manuscript body corresponding to the reference list.

9

Before submission, authors must ensure that all guidance or placeholder text from the template has been completely removed.

Author Declaration

Terms of Submission

Submitting an article signifies that the work presented has not been previously published (except as an abstract, a published lecture, or an academic thesis), is not currently under review by any other publication, has been approved for submission by all authors and, where applicable, by the relevant institutional authorities, and that, upon acceptance, it will not be published elsewhere in the same form, in English or any other language, whether in print or electronic format, without the written consent of the copyright holder. To ensure adherence to these conditions, the article may be subject to verification using originality or duplication detection software.

Please read the declaration carefully. All submissions will be verified for originality and duplication.
