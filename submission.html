Paper Submission

ICCT-SD-26 invites researchers, practitioners, and students worldwide to submit previously unpublished original papers for consideration for presentation at the conference in English.

Submission Guidelines

1

Peer review will be applied to every submission.

2

Providing a uniform format for papers that appear in the conference proceedings is the aim of the conference template.

3

The conference templates include instructions on how to write and format conference papers.

4

It is required that authors utilize the conference manuscript templates listed below.

5

All the submitted papers will undergo plagiarism check by Turnitin/ Ithenticate before undergoing review process.

6

Papers with similarity score more than 20% will not be considered for review and rejected directly.

7

Please make sure that before submitting your conference paper to the conference, all guidance text has been eliminated.

Author Declaration

Terms of Submission

Submitting an article signifies that the work presented has not been previously published (except as an abstract, a published lecture, or an academic thesis), is not currently under review by any other publication, has been approved for submission by all authors and, where applicable, by the relevant institutional authorities, and that, upon acceptance, it will not be published elsewhere in the same form, in English or any other language, whether in print or electronic format, without the written consent of the copyright holder. To ensure adherence to these conditions, the article may be subject to verification using originality or duplication detection software.

Please read the declaration carefully. All submissions will be verified for originality and duplication.
