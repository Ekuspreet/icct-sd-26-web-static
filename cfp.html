Scope

The International Conference on Intelligent Computational and Communication Technologies for Sustainable Development (ICCT-SD 2026) focuses on integrating intelligent computational methods and advanced communication technologies to address global sustainability challenges. It provides a platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to exchange knowledge and present innovations that support the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The scope covers Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Systems, Big Data Analytics and Cloud Computing, Next-Generation Networks, Security and Privacy, and other emerging technologies. Emphasis is placed on sustainable solutions for sectors such as healthcare, agriculture, urban development, manufacturing, and climate resilience. The conference aims to foster collaboration, promote technological innovation, and translate research into practical applications, contributing to a more sustainable and equitable future through interdisciplinary engagement and knowledge dissemination.
