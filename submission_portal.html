Ready To Submit?

Make sure you have prepared your manuscript according to the guidelines and have all necessary documents ready before proceeding to the submission portal.

Submission Guidelines for Microsoft CMT and Peer Review Process.

1. Register on the conference's online submission system i.e. Microsoft CMT.

2. Paper format should be PDF only.

3. Remove author names, affiliations, acknowledgements, and self-citations that reveal identity for double-blind review process.

4. Submit the anonymized PDF file according to the instructions.

5. Enter the title, abstract, keywords, author details (only in metadata, not in the manuscript)

6. Choose relevant conference tracks and subjects.

7. Verify submission completeness and receive confirmation email.
