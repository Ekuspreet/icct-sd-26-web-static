About ICCT-SD 2026

The International Conference on Intelligent Computational and Communication Technologies for Sustainable Development (ICCT-SD 2026) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to explore how emerging technologies can drive progress toward achieving the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The conference focuses on the intersection of intelligent computational methods, advanced communication technologies, and sustainable development, fostering collaboration and innovation in these critical areas.

The primary objective is to address global challenges related to physical and mental health, societal needs, conductive environment, and technological innovations in various fields by leveraging cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and big data analytics. Participants will discuss and present research findings, case studies, and best practices that demonstrate how these technologies can be applied to create sustainable solutions.

By providing a platform for knowledge exchange and networking, the conference aims to inspire actionable insights and partnerships that contribute to the global agenda of sustainable development. The ICCT-SD seeks to catalyze technological innovations that align with the SDGs, ensuring that technological advancements benefit society at large and contribute to a more equitable, sustainable future. This conference is focused on the advanced technologies like Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Systems, Big Data Analytics and Cloud Computing, Next-Generation Network and Communication Technologies, Security and Privacy, and other Contemporary Technologies and their contribution in the various United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals.

Objectives

Promote interdisciplinary research and innovation for sustainable development.

Provide a global platform to share research and best practices aligned with UN SDGs.

Foster academia–industry–policy collaborations on key challenges.

Encourage sustainable digital ecosystems and scalable solutions.

Inspire strategies and partnerships for real-world impact.

The City, Ludhiana.

About Ludhiana

Ludhiana, often called the Manchester of India, is the largest city in Punjab and a major industrial hub of northern India. Known for its vibrant textile industry, hosiery exports, and entrepreneurial spirit, Ludhiana blends tradition with progress. Beyond its economic prowess, the city is rich in Punjabi culture — from lively folk dances and sumptuous cuisine to warm hospitality. The Sutlej River runs along its northern edge, offering a serene contrast to the city’s bustling life. Whether you’re walking through old bazaars, exploring Gurudwaras, or enjoying a glass of lassi at a roadside dhaba, Ludhiana promises an experience full of heart and energy.

Destinations Around Ludhiana

  • Gurudwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib – A sacred Sikh shrine known for its peaceful atmosphere and historical importance.
  • Punjab Agricultural University Museum – A glimpse into Punjab’s rural heritage, traditional crafts, and agricultural evolution.
  • Nehru Rose Garden – A sprawling urban park home to over 1,600 varieties of roses, ideal for picnics and evening strolls.
  • Phillaur Fort – A historical fort built by Maharaja Ranjit Singh, showcasing Sikh military architecture and British influences.
  • Rakh Bagh Park – A beloved recreational spot featuring lush greenery, a toy train, and spaces for families to unwind.
  • Hardy’s World – Punjab’s famous amusement and water park, perfect for thrill-seekers and family fun.
