Awards & Recognition

ICCT-SD 2026 recognizes excellence in research and presentation through various award categories. Outstanding contributions to the field of intelligent computational and communication technologies will be honored.

Best Paper Award

Eligibility:

All nominations will be selected during the review process and grouped into a few key areas.

Nominated papers in the same key area will be presented in the same session.

Only full paper submissions which are presented at the conference will be eligible for the best paper award.

Best Student Paper Award

Eligibility:

The first author of the paper should be a registered university student.

Only full paper submissions which are presented at the conference will be considered.

Best Oral Presentation Award

Eligibility:

For every session, one best oral presentation will be selected by session chairs.

Selection based on originality, applicability, technical merit, PPT quality, and more.
