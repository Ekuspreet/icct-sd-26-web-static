Important Information

To participate in the ICCT-SD-2026, at least one of the authors of each accepted paper must register and pay the applicable fee as specified below. The registration fee includes a conference kit (for offline participants only), access to all technical sessions, workshops, plenary talks, as well as refreshment / meals. Certificates will be issued to all registered participants.

Publication: Only papers that are both accepted and presented will be published in the conference proceedings. Each paper is limited to 8-10 pages.

Students: Students and research scholars are required to submit a scanned copy of their institutional ID card along with the registration form. The original ID card must be presented at the time of paper presentation.
