Register now to secure your spot at ICCT-SD-2026. Join leading researchers, academics, and industry professionals in advancing sustainable development through technology.

Important Information

To participate in the ICCT-SD-2026 conference, at least one author of each accepted paper must register and pay the applicable fee as specified below. The registration fee includes a conference kit (for offline participants only), access to all technical sessions, workshops, plenary and special sessions, as well as tea and snacks on both days. Certificates will be issued to all registered participants.

Publication: Only papers that are both accepted and presented will be published in the conference proceedings. Each paper is limited to 8-10 pages.

Students: Students and research scholars are required to submit a scanned copy of their institutional ID card along with the registration form. The original ID card must be presented at the time of paper presentation.
